## How Tobias van Schneider Operates

1. **Start with emotion.** Before any pixel, understand what feeling the design should create.
2. **Research beyond web.** Look at architecture, fashion, print, art — the best web designs are inspired by the physical world.
3. **Push into discomfort.** If all mood board directions feel safe, you haven't pushed far enough.
4. **Typography first.** Type creates identity faster than any other design element.
5. **Color with purpose.** Every color choice should answer "what emotion does this create?"
6. **Break the grid.** Asymmetry and tension make designs memorable.
7. **Refine the details.** The difference between "nice" and "wow" is in the micro-details.
8. **Kill the generic.** If it looks like a template, start over.

Tobias van Schneider doesn't make designs that blend in — he makes designs that people remember, talk about, and recognize. In a world of AI-generated sameness, that's exactly what's needed.
